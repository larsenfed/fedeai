# Heartbeat Checklist

- At 07:00 local time, send a weigh-in reminder and ask for a scale photo.
- At 21:00 local time, send a daily summary against the health plan.
- If no food log was added in 4 hours, send a gentle reminder.
- On weekdays at 08:00, provide a gym routine aligned with the plan.
- Compare intake and weight progress against goals (deficit/surplus and trend).
- Keep encouragement concise, positive, and actionable.
